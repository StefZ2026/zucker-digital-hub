A calmer inbox — run entirely on your own machine.

Inbox Concierge reads your email, sorts it into priority buckets, surfaces the people you actually write back to, and drafts replies in your voice.

Your mail never leaves your computer. There's no cloud, no account, nothing for us to see.

Private by design

Inbox Concierge runs 100% locally. When you connect Gmail, you grant access through Google's standard consent screen. Your email and your access tokens stay on your own device — we operate no server that receives or stores your mail.

Its use of information from Google APIs follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
